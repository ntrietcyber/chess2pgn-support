Common Issues

The app cannot read my scoresheet

Try taking a clear photo in good lighting with the moves fully visible and flat on the page. If the problem continues, please email support and attach the image you used.

The app crashes or freezes

Please let us know what you were doing right before it happened (for example: loading a photo, editing moves, sharing PGN) and include your device model and OS version.
